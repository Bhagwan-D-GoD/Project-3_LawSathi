LAW SAATHI
Elevate Your Legal Requirements with Intelligent Research Tools
Unlock the full potential of your legal requirements with our intelligent research tools. Our application provides comprehensive resources and cutting-edge features to enhance your legal research, needs and streamline your workflow.

Features

AI Powered Chatbot

Our AI-powered chatbot has been trained on different rulebooks of Nepal and is able to answer most of your queries regarding the law of Nepal successfully.

  • 24/7 Availability of the chatbot to answer your queries.
  • Easy to use with a simple and intuitive interface for seamless interaction.
  • Equipped with extensive information on various laws and regulations, including civil, criminal, corporate, and family law.

Lawyer Search

Users can select different parameters for lawyers and get shown the best-rated lawyers.

  • The feature showcases top-rated lawyers based on user reviews and ratings, ensuring users find highly qualified and reputable legal professionals.
  • Users can directly contact selected lawyers through the platform, facilitating easy communication and consultation scheduling.

Video Appointment

Schedule video appointments with selected lawyers.

  • Users can easily schedule video appointments with lawyers at their convenience, avoiding the need for in-person visits.
  • Accessible from any device with an internet connection, including smartphones, tablets, and computers.
  • Ensures high-definition video and clear audio for a seamless communication experience.

News Portal

Stay updated with the latest worldwide news.

  • Provides up-to-date news on recent legal developments, case verdicts, and changes in laws and regulations.
  • Offers real-time news updates, ensuring users are always informed about the latest happenings in the legal world.

About Us

Welcome to Law Saathi!

We are a dedicated team of four final-year Computer Engineering students, guided by our experienced supervisor. This web application is the culmination of our final year project, aimed at making legal assistance accessible and user-friendly.

Our Vision

Inspired by the difficulties people face in navigating legal matters, we set out to create a platform that bridges the gap between legal knowledge and those in need of it. Our goal is to make legal processes simpler and more transparent.

Our Journey

Developing Law Saathi was both challenging and rewarding. We tackled complex legal requirements, user experience design, and data security issues head-on, leveraging modern technology to build a robust and reliable platform.

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuously improving Law Saathi, ensuring it remains a valuable resource for everyone seeking legal assistance. Thank you for being part of our journey.
